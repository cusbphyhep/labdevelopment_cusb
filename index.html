Research Areas

🔬

Neutrino Physics

Advanced neutrino detection, oscillation studies, and sterile neutrino searches using cutting-edge detector technologies.

NOvA collaboration DUNE R&D Texono experiment
🌌

Dark Matter Research

Direct detection methods, underground experiments, and novel detector development for WIMP and axion searches.

Pire-GAMEDARK Xenon detector R&D Background studies
⚡

Cosmic Ray Physics

High-energy cosmic ray detection, extensive air shower studies, and muon tomography applications.

Muon tomography Space weather studies

Next-generation detector technology, electronics development, and novel readout systems for HEP experiments.
